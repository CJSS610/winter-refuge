Meet Our Speaker

Pastor John Smith

Youth Pastor, Grace Community Church

Pastor John has been serving in youth ministry for over 15 years and has a passion for helping students discover their identity in Christ.

He has spoken at numerous youth conferences and retreats across the country, and is known for his engaging teaching style that connects biblical truth with the real-life challenges that students face today.

When he's not teaching, Pastor John enjoys hiking, playing guitar, and spending time with his wife and three kids.

Retreat Message Series

"Who You Are in Christ"

Throughout the weekend, Pastor John will lead us through a powerful series exploring our identity in Christ. In a world that constantly tries to define who we should be, we'll discover what God's Word says about who we truly are as His children.

Session 1: Loved

Understanding God's unconditional love for us

Session 2: Chosen

What it means to be selected by God for His purpose

Session 3: Transformed

Living out our new identity in daily life
